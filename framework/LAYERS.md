# Layers

Layers are independent specification manifests that together form a coherent application. Each layer answers a specific question and receives requirements from its own prompt or intake. Upstream material provides context for coherence and traceability, not replacement intent.

## Layer Independence

Each layer carries its own voice. It honors upstream perspective for coherence while protecting the origin of its own requirements. Clarity of origin preserves accountability and prevents unintended derivation.

## Upstream References

References exist to illuminate relationships and lineage. They communicate how intent, behavior, technology, environment, and verification depend on one another without turning context into a second source of requirements.

## Layer Order

Layers accumulate context in a gentle progression: business intent informs functional behavior, which informs stack choices. Infrastructure interprets the combined understanding for operation, and Coverage surveys everything to confirm completeness. Order aids understanding rather than dictating derivation.

## Layer Definitions

### Business — Why?
Captures intent, value, and outcomes. Describes actors, goals, and success signals in human terms.

### Functional — What?
Expresses behaviors, contracts, and flows that realize the intent. Frames how experiences and data interact while staying anchored to business meaning.

### Stack — With what?
Explores tooling and technical approaches that can support the desired behaviors. Balances preferences, constraints, and rationale without overstepping into deployment concerns.

### Infrastructure — Where?
Considers where the system runs and how it is operated. Connects environment concerns with the intent and behaviors defined earlier.

### Coverage — What is verified?
Ensures every expectation has a path to verification. Maps acceptance ideas to ways they can be observed and measured.

## Dependency Notes

Layers remain independent yet coherent. Business, Functional, and Stack progress linearly inside development work; Infrastructure aligns that trio with the execution environment; Coverage synthesizes all layers to confirm completeness.
